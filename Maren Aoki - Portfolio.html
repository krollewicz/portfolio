About

(02)   The person
MA

For nine years I've helped startups and studios turn fuzzy ideas into products people actually keep using. Design lead, sometimes the whole design team, always hands-on.

Approach

I start with the verb — what is someone trying to do — then strip everything that gets in the way. Prototype early, test in the real device, ship, and refine in daylight.

I think in systems but obsess over the single most important screen. Motion is a tool for clarity, never confetti.
